Delete Account

Warning: Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. This action will remove all your data from our systems.

What Happens When You Delete Your Account

  • Your profile information will be permanently deleted
  • Your reward history will be anonymized
  • Any unused rewards will be forfeited
  • You will no longer be able to access the app with this account

Before You Delete Your Account

Consider the following alternatives:

  • Temporary Deactivation: Contact us if you prefer to temporarily disable your account
  • Privacy Settings: Adjust your privacy settings instead of deleting your account
  • Unsubscribe: Opt out of marketing emails without deleting your account

How to Delete Your Account

To proceed with account deletion:

  1. Open the QR Reward System mobile app
  2. Go to Settings â†’ Account
  3. Tap on "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm your decision by entering your password
  5. Complete any verification steps required

Alternatively, you can request account deletion by emailing us at support@qrrewardsystem.com from the email associated with your account.

Data Retention After Deletion

Please note that some information may be retained in our backups for a limited time (up to 90 days) before being permanently erased. Certain transaction data may be retained for legal and accounting purposes but will be disassociated from your personal information.
